# Chicago Race Market Intel
*Generated: 2026-04-17 | For: US Enterprise Sales Trip*

## Why Chicago

- Home of Bank of America Chicago Marathon — 1 of 7 World Marathon Majors
- ~50,000 runners/year, $277M economic impact per event
- Deep running culture — multiple races year-round (5Ks, halfs, triathlons, charity runs)
- Race organizers are professional operations — they care about data, conversion, sponsor metrics

## What US Race Organizers Care About

- **Revenue share transparency** — sophisticated buyers, will ask about take rates
- **Timing system integration** — ChronoTrack, Race Roster, RunSignUp are dominant; don't compete with their timing stack
- **CCPA compliance** — data privacy is a real concern for enterprise US organizers
- **USD pricing + Stripe** — non-negotiable standard
- **Photo turnaround speed** — same day or faster expected
