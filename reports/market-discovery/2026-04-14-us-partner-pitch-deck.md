## Slide 5 — Revenue Sharing Model

**Every photo sale is split three ways — automatically via Stripe.**

```
Runner pays $9.99
        │
        ▼
┌────────────────────────────────────┐
│        FindMyShots Platform        │
│  Stripe processes + splits payout  │
└──────┬─────────────┬───────────────┘
       │             │              │
       ▼             ▼              ▼
  FMS (20%)   Organizer (20%)  Photographer (60%)
   ~$2.00         ~$2.00           ~$6.00
```

*After Stripe processing fee (~$0.59)*

### For Bundle Sales ($19.99):

| Party | Share | Per Bundle Sold |
|---|---|---|

This organically pushes conversion from ~10% → 15%+, generating more revenue for all three parties.

### Why Photographers Get 60%
- Better than shooting for free
- Better than a flat day rate
- Scalable recurring income — earn every time a runner buys your photo
- FMS handles platform, payments, search, and customer support

## Slide 8 — How Payouts Work

**Technology:** Stripe Connect — industry-standard automated payout infrastructure

**Flow:**
1. Runner pays at checkout → Stripe collects payment
2. Stripe automatically splits payment three ways in real-time:
   - Photographer's 60% → direct to their connected bank account
   - Organizer's 20% → direct to their connected bank account
   - FMS's 20% → FMS account
3. Payouts on agreed schedule (weekly, per event, or monthly)
4. Everyone sees earnings in real-time on their dashboard

**No manual invoicing. No waiting. No chasing payments.**

Onboarding takes 15 minutes — connect a bank account once, every sale after that is automatic.

## Slide 9 — Tax Handling

### Sales Tax — US Target Markets

| City | State | Digital Downloads Taxable? | Rate |
|---|---|---|---|
| San Francisco | California | ❌ **NOT taxable** | $0 |
| Los Angeles | California | ❌ **NOT taxable** | $0 |
| All of California | California | ❌ **NOT taxable** | $0 |
| Chicago | Illinois | ✅ Taxable | 6.25% + local |

**California — our primary market — has zero sales tax on digital downloads.** No collection, no registration, no threshold to worry about.

**Illinois (Chicago):** Sales tax applies after FMS reaches $100,000 in cumulative annual sales to Illinois customers (across all Chicago events, full calendar year). Below that threshold — no obligation.

### How Sales Tax Works for Partners
- Sales tax is collected from the **runner** — not from organizers or photographers
- It is added **on top** of the purchase price at checkout
- It does **not reduce partner payouts** — the 20/60 split is unaffected
- Stripe Tax handles calculation and collection automatically
- FMS remits to the state — partners have no involvement

**Example (Chicago, Illinois):**
```
Runner pays: $10.61
  ├─ $9.99  → revenue split (FMS 20% / Organizer 20% / Photographer 60%)
  └─ $0.62  → held by Stripe → remitted to Illinois by FMS
```

### Income Tax — Each Party's Own Responsibility
| Party | What They Pay Tax On | Where |
|---|---|---|
| Runner | Nothing (buyer) | — |
| **Organizer** | Their 20% share | US income/business tax |
| **Photographer** | Their 60% share | US self-employment tax |
| FMS | Its 20% platform fee | Philippine corporate tax |

Each party handles their own income tax on what they receive. FMS issues **1099-NEC** annually to any US partner earning $600+/year — Stripe Connect collects the required tax ID during onboarding automatically.
