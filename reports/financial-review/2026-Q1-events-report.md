## Key Observations

1. **Event volume grew fast, revenue grew slow.** Jan: 20 events, Feb: 47, Mar: 53 — but GMV only grew from ₱3.3M → ₱4.6M. More small events diluting the average.
2. **Average revenue per event dropped sharply.** ₱165K → ₱89K → ₱86K. Volume strategy is working for reach, not for revenue per event.
3. **Conversion rate is low but stable.** ~1.1% across Q1. Massive room to grow — 8M photos uploaded, only 91K sold.
4. **Revenue is concentrated in top events.** In every month, top 5 events = ~70–80% of GMV. Long tail of small community runs contribute very little.
5. **14 events had ₱0 revenue** across Q1 — photos shot but nothing sold. Worth investigating photographer/buyer experience at these events.
6. **Take rate holding at ~24%.** Consistent. Will shift significantly when Rio Phase 2 (45% take rate) kicks in.
